That person uses Public transports if: their private vehicle is unavailable and the destination is inaccessible by soft modes, Use Private modes if: they need to maintain flexibility for their activities and travel directly to their destination without transfers, Use Soft modes if: the trip is very short, direct, and the difficulty of finding parking at the destination outweighs the convenience of driving. This choice is deeply ingrained, as they do not reconsider their travel habits and perceive public transportation as overly restrictive and its schedules as difficult to understand.
